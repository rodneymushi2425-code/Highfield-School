Welcome to Highfield International Open School

Highfield International Open School is a fully registered tuition centre under the Institute of Adult Education.


We offer Non-formal Secondary Education through both local and international curricula including NECTA QT, O-Level, A-Level, Cambridge IGCSE, Cambridge AS and Cambridge A2 programmes.
